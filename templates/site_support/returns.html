1. Overview

We want you to be satisfied with your order. If you need to return a product, please follow the steps below. This page describes the general return process and refund handling.

2. Return Eligibility

  • The product should be returned in good condition (unless it arrived damaged).
  • Where applicable, return the product with its original packaging and all included items.
  • We may refuse returns if the product is used, damaged after delivery, or incomplete.

3. Return Period

You can request a return within a specific period after delivery (example: 14 days). The exact return period may depend on your location and the type of product.

Return request period: 14 days after delivery (example).

4. How to Request a Return

  1. Submit a return request via our Inquiry form.
  2. Include your order number and a short description of the issue.
  3. If the product is damaged or incorrect, attach photos (recommended).
  4. We will reply with instructions and the return address (if applicable).

Open Inquiry form →

5. Return Shipping

  • Return shipping costs may be paid by the customer unless the return is due to our mistake (wrong/damaged item).
  • Please use secure packaging to prevent damage during shipping.
  • Keep the shipping receipt / tracking number until the return is completed.

6. Refunds

  • After receiving and inspecting the returned product, we will confirm the return status.
  • Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method when possible.
  • Refund processing time depends on the payment provider/bank.

Refund processing time: 3–10 business days (example).

7. Damaged / Wrong Item

  • If your package arrived damaged, take photos of the packaging and the product.
  • If you received a wrong item, please contact us with your order number.
  • We will provide a solution (replacement, partial refund, or full refund depending on the case).

You may also review our Delivery page for shipping details.

8. Exceptions

Some products may not be eligible for return (example: custom-made/personalized items), unless they arrive damaged or incorrect.

If you are unsure, contact us before placing an order.

9. Contact

If you have questions about returns, refunds, or a specific order, contact us via our Inquiry form.
