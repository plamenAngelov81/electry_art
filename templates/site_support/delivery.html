1. Order Processing

  • Orders are processed after payment confirmation (if applicable).
  • Processing time depends on product availability and workload.
  • If we need additional details, we may contact you before shipping.

Typical processing time: 1–3 business days (example).

2. Delivery Methods

We offer delivery through courier services and/or pickup points, depending on your location.

  • Courier to address — home/office delivery.
  • Pickup point / locker — collect your package at a convenient location.

Available methods are shown during checkout based on your address.

3. Delivery Time

  • Estimated delivery time starts after the order is processed and shipped.
  • Delivery time may vary during holidays or peak periods.

Estimated delivery: 1–5 business days (example).

4. Delivery Fees

  • Delivery fees depend on your location, shipping method, and order size/weight.
  • The exact fee is shown at checkout before you confirm the order.

Free delivery: available for orders above a certain amount (optional).

5. Tracking

  • When available, we provide tracking information after the order is shipped.
  • You can check status via the courier tracking page.

6. Failed Delivery / Wrong Address

  • Please ensure your delivery address and phone number are correct.
  • If a package is returned due to incorrect address or failed delivery attempts, we will contact you.
  • Additional shipping costs may apply for resending the package.

7. Damaged Package

  • If the package is visibly damaged, please take photos and contact us as soon as possible.
  • Keep the packaging until the issue is resolved (couriers may request inspection).

If you received a damaged product, please also review our Returns policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about delivery, you can contact us via our Inquiry form.
